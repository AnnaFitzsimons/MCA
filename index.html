Project Dataset: Musical Theater

Week 1: Musical Datasets

Current manifestations of this dataset theme include a "Broadway" Genre on Spotify. This contains playlists such as:

  • Best of Broadway
  • Sucessos da Broadway
  • Hits from Musicals
  • Ballads from Musicals

The app also possesses several unofficial playlists made by spotify users


How is the data presented, described and analysed?

The data is presented in a series of playlists, each accompanied by a cover photo as well as the number of followers the playlist has. Compared to other genres "Broadway" has significantly less content within it, suggesting a lack of demand. Each song that exists within the playlist comes with 'Song Credits' which includes who the song is 'Performed by'; 'Written by'; 'Produced by'; as well as the 'Source' of the music.
Spotify presumably produces these genre playlists by analysing each song's accompanying metadata.
