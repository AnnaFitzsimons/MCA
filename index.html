Week 8: Music as Sound

For Week 8 we downloaded three music tracks of different genres from Free Music Archive

Following this I constructed a table within Excel to showcase the different metadata associate with each track. I later changed copied this table into a word document which contains Waveforms and Spectogram images for each track: Metadata Document

Time Frequency V Waveform Analysis: In evaluation of the difference between Time Frequency analysis and Waveform it could argued that Time Frequency has a certain advantage over waveform due to its simplicity. Time frequency analysis can be read far easier than that of waveform which can often be impossible to decipher any information from. With reference to the the spectograms and waveforms included in the document above, in the comparrison of the spectograms of 'Grass is not a flower' and 'Positive and Fun' it could be argued that is for more easier to draw information from them. For example, in looking at the end of both pieces of audio we can see that 'Positive and Fun' comes to a conclusion at a much higher amplitude than that on 'Grass is not a flower', this can be determined through the darkness or brightness of the waves. Therefore due to the more simplistic nature of Time Frequency analysis it could be argued that we are able to gain greater detail than we would be permitted through that of waveform.
